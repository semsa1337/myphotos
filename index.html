About Me

Hi, I’m Dimitrije, a curious person who loves exploring new things. My biggest interests are photography and cars – passions that grew naturally through friends who first introduced me to them.

For me, photography is about discovering new perspectives, while cars represent creativity, style, and a sense of freedom. Even though I’m still figuring out exactly where this path will lead, I enjoy learning and growing through every new experience. My goal is to build a small side career doing what I love, while always staying open to new ideas and inspiration.
